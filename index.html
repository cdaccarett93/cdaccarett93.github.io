Glucose-Guardian

— Fresno State Hackathon 2017

This web application's purpose is to provide a functional calculation that will yield exact amounts of the fast acting sugar source (skittles) to bring your levels back to normal. It has the option to automatically pull your diabetic profiles, and current blood glucose level from the NightScout API. Also has a geolocation text message feature if blood sugar reaches a dangerous level.
